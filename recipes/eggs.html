Eggs on the Grill

Grilled eggs on a open fire

Just spray the pan and crack the eggs and put them on the grill.

Add some chopped onions and peppers for fun!

Ingredients

  • A dozen eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat.
  2. Coat all holes of a muffin pan with cooking spray and crack an egg into each hole.
  3. Place on grill and grill over medium heat for 2 minutes, or to desired doneness.
